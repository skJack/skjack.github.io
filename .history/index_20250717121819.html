Biography

I am currently a last year Ph.D student in the Department of Artificial Intelligence, School of Informatics, Xiamen University, advised by Prof. Rongrong Ji.

My research interests include Face Forgery Detection, Multimodal Learning, AIGC Detection, Camouflaged Object Detection, and related areas. My research goal is to make AIGC more controllable and manageable.
